Short Bio

I am a Research Associate at the Chinese Information Processing Laboratory, Institute of Software, Chinese Academy of Sciences.

My research interests center on Natural Language Processing, such as Information Extraction, Word Representation, and Discourse Analysis.

  • Unified Structure Generation for Universal Information Extraction. Yaojie Lu, Qing Liu, Dai Dai, Xinyan Xiao, Hongyu Lin, Xianpei Han, Le Sun and Hua Wu. ACL 2022. [link]
  • End-to-End Neural Event Coreference Resolution. Yaojie Lu, Hongyu Lin, Jialong Tang, Xianpei Han and Le Sun. Artificial Intelligence, Volume 303, February 2022, 103632. [link]
  • Text2Event: Controllable Sequence-to-Structure Generation for End-to-end Event Extraction. Yaojie Lu, Hongyu Lin, Jin Xu, Xianpei Han, Jialong Tang, Annan Li, Le Sun, Meng Liao and Shaoyi Chen. ACL-IJCNLP 2021. [link]
  • A Rigorous Study on Named Entity Recognition: Can Fine-tuning Pretrained Model Lead to the Promised Land? Hongyu Lin, Yaojie Lu, Jialong Tang, Xianpei Han, Le Sun, Zhicheng Wei and Nicholas Jing Yuan. EMNLP 2020. [link]
